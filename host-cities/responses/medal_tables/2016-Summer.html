2016 Rio Medal Tally

Here is the medal tally list for the 2016 Rio Olympics. A total of 306 events were held in Rio, four more than the 302 in London due to the presence of the two new sports, golf and rugby sevens. See more about the Olympics Games in 2016.

This is the medal tally after completion of all events. Nearly 60 countries won at least one gold, and 87 at least any medal. As per tradition, the list order is based on the number of gold medals first, then silver and bronze. The USA would also be leading the medal table if it was sorted by the total medals won, though China would be second on such a list. Compare this table to the predicted medal tally for 2016 - the top two countries predicted to be on the top were USA and China, but Team GB was in the mix too.
