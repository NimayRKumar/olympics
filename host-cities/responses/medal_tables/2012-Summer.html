2012 London Medal Tally

This is the final medal tally list after completion 2012 the London Olympics. A total of 85 Countries won at least one medal. As per tradition, the list order is based first on the number of gold medals, then silver and bronze.

However, this list may not be up to date, as sometimes athletes have their medals taken away years after the event. Even in 2019, there had been more than 60 athletes disqualified from the London 2012 Olympic Games under the International Olympic Committee's sample retesting program, many of these medal winners.

This list has been updated after the shot putter from Belarus was stripped of her gold medal after the Olympics for doping, elevating New Zealand to first place in that event. There are probably more changes needed.
